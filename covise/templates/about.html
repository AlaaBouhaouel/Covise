Our Story
We built Covise because finding the right co-founder in the GCC shouldn't be left to luck.

Finding a serious business partner in the Gulf is genuinely hard. There is no structured place to look, no way to verify who is truly committed, and no process to test compatibility before you go all in. Most partnerships don't fail because of bad ideas - they fail because of bad alignment.

We watched talented founders across Saudi Arabia and the UAE either build alone out of frustration, or rush into the wrong partnerships because there was nowhere serious to meet people. LinkedIn wasn't built for this. Incubators only serve a small slice. And most platforms that claim to help are either too niche, too passive, or too focused on deep tech to be useful to the full spectrum of entrepreneurs in the region.

Covise was built to fix that.
A verified, structured, AI-powered network where serious founders find each other - across every industry, every city in the Gulf, and across borders. Not just tech. Not just the well-funded. Everyone who is serious about building something real.
