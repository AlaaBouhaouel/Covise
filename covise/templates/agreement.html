Purpose-driven participation

This network is for founders, operators, and builders actively working on ventures or committed to starting one.

Execution over conversation

CoVise exists to create real outcomes. Connections here should lead to action, not just talk.

Integrity in representation

Present your background, experience, and intentions accurately. Misrepresentation undermines trust.

Purposeful communication

Reach out with clarity and relevance. Vague outreach and mass messaging do not belong here.

Zero tolerance for noise

Spam, unsolicited promotion, and irrelevant outreach have no place in this network.

Confidentiality and respect

Ideas, conversations, and private information shared here should be handled with discretion.

Depth over volume

Meaningful relationships with the right people matter more than a large number of surface-level connections.

No hate or discrimination

Discriminatory, hateful, or offensive content toward any person or group is not tolerated.

No political or divisive content

CoVise is a professional building space. Political, religious, and divisive content is out of scope.

No misinformation or rumours

Communicate with accuracy and responsibility. Do not spread false or unverified claims.

Strictly no illegal activity

Fraud, IP theft, data misuse, misconduct, or illegal solicitation can result in permanent removal.

No harassment or intimidation

Threats, harassment, or intimidatory behavior in public or private communication will not be tolerated.
