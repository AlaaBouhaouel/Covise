Community Guidelines

Before you enter CoVise, please review the standards for posting, outreach, and collaboration on the platform.

1

Purpose-driven participation

This network is for founders, operators, and builders actively working on ventures or committed to starting one. Participation should be grounded in clear intent, real ambition, and serious contribution to the ecosystem.

2

Execution over conversation

CoVise exists to create real outcomes. Conversations, introductions, and collaborations should move toward action, progress, and measurable work rather than vague networking for its own sake.

3

Integrity in representation

Present your background, experience, and intentions accurately. Misrepresentation of your identity, capabilities, traction, or goals undermines trust and damages the quality of the network for everyone.

4

Purposeful communication

Reach out with clarity and relevance. Outreach should be specific, context-aware, and respectful of the other person's time. Vague messaging, generic asks, and low-effort contact do not belong here.

5

Zero tolerance for noise

Spam, unsolicited promotion, repetitive posting, and irrelevant outreach have no place in this network. Members are expected to add signal, not clutter, to the platform experience.

6

Confidentiality and respect

Ideas, conversations, and private information shared here should be handled with discretion. Respect boundaries, permissions, and the trust that comes with founder-to-founder communication.

7

Depth over volume

Meaningful relationships with the right people matter more than a high number of shallow interactions. The goal is thoughtful alignment, not mass connection collecting.

8

No hate or discrimination

Discriminatory, hateful, or offensive content toward any person or group is not tolerated. Every member should be able to participate without fear of exclusion, hostility, or abuse.

9

No political or divisive content

CoVise is a professional building space. Political, religious, and other divisive content that distracts from venture formation and constructive collaboration is out of scope.

10

No misinformation or rumours

Communicate with accuracy and responsibility. Do not spread false, exaggerated, or unverified claims about people, companies, fundraising, opportunities, or platform activity.

11

Strictly no illegal activity

Fraud, IP theft, data misuse, misconduct, illegal solicitation, and any other unlawful behavior can result in immediate and permanent removal from the platform.

12

No harassment or intimidation

Threats, harassment, coercion, intimidation, or abusive behavior in public or private communication will not be tolerated. Professional respect is a baseline requirement for participation.

Before You Continue

Confirm your agreement

By continuing, you confirm that you understand these standards and will follow them while participating in the CoVise network.
