Core Features

Built for serious founders to align faster, validate better, and execute with confidence.

Verified Profiles

Every founder is identity-verified before appearing in the network. No fake accounts, no passive browsers.

Behavioral Assessment

A proprietary questionnaire that scores founders on commitment level, risk tolerance, leadership style, and execution history - producing a Founder Conviction score that powers your matches.

AI Alignment Matching

Our algorithm matches founders on complementary skills, vision alignment, working style, and psychological fit - not just job titles or industries.

Trial Build

Before committing to a partnership, founders complete a short AI-suggested collaborative task together. See how you think, decide, and execute as a team before it matters.

AI Research Assistant

Built-in tools for market research, financial estimation, legal overviews, and operational planning - lowering the barrier for first-time founders.

Cross-Border Network

Connect GCC founders with global partners and foreign founders seeking GCC market entry. Not country-locked.

Founder Events & Meetups (Coming Soon)

Exclusive in-person and virtual events for verified Covise members across the GCC.
