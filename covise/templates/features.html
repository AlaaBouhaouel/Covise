Core Features

Built to help founders align faster - and execute with more confidence.

Covise is designed for serious founders who need more than profiles and messaging. Every feature is structured to reduce guesswork, surface compatibility early, and help strong teams form with clarity.

Instead of acting like another passive directory, the platform combines verification, behavioral signal, structured matching, and execution support so founder relationships can be assessed before major decisions are made.

"The goal is not just to help founders meet. It is to help the right founders know whether they should actually build together."

Verified Profiles

Every founder is identity-verified before appearing in the network. No fake accounts, no passive browsers.

Behavioral Assessment

A proprietary questionnaire that scores founders on commitment level, risk tolerance, leadership style, and execution history, producing a conviction signal that powers matches.

AI Alignment Matching

Our algorithm matches founders on complementary skills, vision alignment, working style, and psychological fit, not just job titles or industries.

Trial Build

Before committing to a partnership, founders complete a short AI-suggested collaborative task together so they can see how they think, decide, and execute as a team.

AI Research Assistant

Built-in tools for market research, financial estimation, legal overviews, and operational planning, lowering the barrier for first-time founders.

Cross-Border Network

Connect GCC founders with global partners and foreign founders seeking GCC market entry. The network is intentionally not country-locked.

Founder Events & Meetups

Exclusive in-person and virtual events for verified Covise members across the GCC.
